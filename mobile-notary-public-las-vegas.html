Why consider a Traveling / Mobile Notary?

"We are available when you need us"

We will travel to your home or business, hospitals, nursing homes or anywhere you would like to meet at your convenience to notarize your documents.

We have helped many individuals, direct lenders, escrow companies and title companies and have successfully closed over {{ site.stat_loans | thousands_separated }} loans. These loans have included purchases, fixed and adjustable rate loans, refinances, home equity loans, loan modifications, e-signings and more.

A #1 Document Services Notaries Public are certified, background checked, bonded and insured. We carry a $10,000 bond and $100,000 in Errors & Omissions insurance.

We are members of the National Notary Association and offer mobile notary services. We specialize in loan and refinance closings to Title/Escrow Services, Lenders and Signing Agencies nationwide.

We accept email, fax and overnight documents. All documents are printed on a laser printer.
